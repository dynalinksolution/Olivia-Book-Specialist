Hi, I’m Olivia, a Book Marketer & Book Strategist.

I founded Olivia Book Specialist to support authors who want to promote their books professionally but feel overwhelmed by the marketing process. I understand that writing a book takes dedication, and my goal is to help ensure that your work gets the visibility it deserves.

I work with authors at different stages of their publishing journey, offering thoughtful, ethical, and personalized book marketing support.

My passion for books and storytelling inspired me to step into the world of book marketing. Over time, I’ve developed a strong interest in helping authors navigate promotion in a way that feels authentic and effective. As a growing book marketer and strategist, I focus on research-driven approaches, continuous learning, and staying up to date with book marketing trends. Every project I take on is handled with care, professionalism, and a commitment to delivering value.

MY MISSION:
To help authors confidently promote their books, build credibility, and grow long-term visibility in the publishing world through ethical and strategic marketing.

MY VALUES:
To be a trusted partner for authors seeking personalized book marketing solutions that align with their unique goals and values.

  1. Integrity: I believe in honest and ethical book promotion. I never use misleading tactics, spam outreach, or shortcuts that could harm your reputation as an author. Every strategy I apply is transparent, respectful, and focused on long-term credibility rather than quick wins.
  2. Personalization: Every author and every book is unique. That’s why I take time to understand your goals, genre, and audience before recommending or implementing any marketing strategy. My services are tailored to fit your specific needs, not a one-size-fits-all approach.
  3. Professionalism: Clear communication and dependable support are central to how I work. I value timely responses, organized processes, and respectful collaboration, so you always feel informed, confident, and supported throughout our work together.
  4. Creativity:Your book deserves thoughtful and creative marketing that reflects its message and voice. I approach each project with fresh ideas and careful planning, ensuring that promotion enhances your story rather than overshadows it.
  5. Growth: The publishing and marketing world is constantly evolving. I am committed to continuous learning, improving my skills, and staying updated with industry trends so I can offer strategies that remain relevant and effective for authors.

WHO I WORK WITH

    I work with:

  • Self-published and indie authors
  • First-time authors
  • Coaches and thought leaders with books
  • Authors seeking professional promotion support

Choosing the right marketing support matters. When you work with me, you get personalized attention, a thoughtful approach to promotion, and a genuine partner who cares about your book’s success.
