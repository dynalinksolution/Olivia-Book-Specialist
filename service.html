Book Marketing Services for Authors

Book Review Outreach

What this service is:
Book reviews help readers trust your book before buying it. With my book review outreach service, I research and contact relevant book reviewers, bloggers, and book-focused platforms that align with your genre and audience.

I create personalized outreach messages and focus on genuine, ethical promotion, no spam, no fake reviews. The goal is to help your book gain authentic reviews that improve credibility and visibility.

This service helps you:

  • Build reader trust and social proof
  • Increase your book’s visibility
  • Strengthen your author credibility

Social Media Marketing & Management

What this service is:
Social media can feel overwhelming for authors. This service helps you build a professional and consistent social media presence that connects you with readers and supports your book promotion goals.

I assist with content planning, posting ideas, scheduling, and audience engagement, focusing on creating content that feels natural, engaging, and aligned with your author brand.

This service helps you:

  • Grow and engage your reader audience
  • Build a recognizable author brand
  • Maintain consistency without stress

Press Release Creation

What this service is:
A press release helps position your book or author brand as professional and newsworthy. I write clear, well-structured press releases for book launches, announcements, and important updates.

Each press release is crafted to highlight your book’s key message while maintaining a professional tone suitable for media outlets, bloggers, and readers.

This service helps you:

  • Increase credibility and professionalism
  • Support book launches and announcements
  • Enhance visibility beyond social media

Ads Creation & Promotion

What this service is:
Advertising allows your book to reach a wider audience when done correctly. With this service, I create compelling ad copy and promotional visuals designed to capture attention and encourage readers to take action.

The focus is on clarity, strong messaging, and aligning ads with your goals, whether that’s increasing awareness, driving traffic, or supporting book sales.

This service helps you:

  • Reach new potential readers
  • Increase book awareness and visibility
  • Promote launches, discounts, or events

Common Questions from Authors

Who are your services best suited for?

My services are ideal for self-published authors, indie authors, first-time authors, and professionals who want to promote their books professionally and build long-term visibility.

Do you work with first-time authors?

Yes. I work with both first-time and experienced authors. I take time to explain the marketing process clearly and guide new authors step by step.

Can you guarantee book sales or reviews?

No marketer can ethically guarantee sales or reviews. What I do guarantee is professional, ethical promotion and strategic efforts designed to improve visibility, credibility, and reach.

How do I know which service is right for my book?

If you’re unsure, I offer a free consultation to understand your goals and recommend the most suitable service for your book and audience.

What genres do you work with?

I work with a wide range of genres. During our consultation, I review your book’s genre and audience to ensure the marketing approach is a good fit.

Do you offer customized packages?

Yes. Services can be tailored based on your goals, timeline, and budget. I focus on flexibility rather than one-size-fits-all solutions.

How long does book marketing take?

Book marketing is not instant. Results vary depending on the service, genre, and goals, but consistent and strategic promotion delivers better long-term outcomes.

Will I need to be actively involved?

Your involvement depends on the service. I handle the marketing work while keeping you informed, and I may request input when needed to ensure accuracy and alignment.

How do I get started?

Simply contact me through the website or book a free consultation. We’ll discuss your book, goals, and the best way to move forward.

Do you work with authors outside my country?

Yes. I work with authors worldwide, as most book marketing services are handled online.

Not Sure Which Service Is Right for You?

Every book has different goals and needs. If you’re unsure which service fits your project, I’ll help you choose the best option based on your book and audience.
